The best reefs in Sharm El Sheikh

Here at Camel Dive Club we offer our unique Reef Discovery Programme. Reef Discovery gives all ages and levels of swimmers the opportunity to get out on the boat and see the beauty of corals and fish under the close guidance of our professional guides.

You will be fitted out with equipment including a floatation device, given clear safety and snorkel site briefings and taken to several amazing locations throughout the day. Your guide will point out all the fish and corals, give you tips on swimming and using the snorkel equipment and make sure that you have an unforgettable experience.
