Your first day

Camel Hotel is a boutique hotel offering thirty-eight comfortable rooms. The rooms are arranged on two floors around a central courtyard with swimming pool. Thirty Pool view rooms have their own terrace or balcony overlooking the pool. Eight standard rooms are of the same quality, but without pool view and without terrace or balcony.

All our rooms have en-suite bathroom, air conditioning, refrigerator, tea and coffee making facilities, satellite TV, and safe deposit box. Free wireless internet access is available throughout all the rooms and public areas of the resort.

Everything under one roof

Camel Dive Club & Hotel is designed to leave you wanting for nothing but a longer trip. We are divers and we know what we want from a diving holiday. Fantastic diving with an experienced, seasoned team, amazing food, somewhere to unwind, laugh, relax, have a drink and be in the moment is what makes a stand out diving holiday.

Taking the very best dive experiences and stand out moments from trips and putting them all in one place is what Camel Dive Club & Hotel has build over the last 36 years and we are proud to say that we provide this unique service where no one else can. Starting with the private airport transfer when you land, enjoying our boutique hotel, relaxing by the custom built pool and Jacuzzi, dining and drinking in the best restaurants and bars Sharm has to offer. All of this and that is not even mentioning the world class, award winning diving center and dive sites that we have right on our doorstep.

Swimming pool

Surrounded by shady parasols and sun loungers, our custom built pool and Jacuzzi is the perfect oasis located in the heart of our boutique hotel and resort. Custom built for diver training as well as relaxation, you can enjoy a cool drink whilst watching students experiencing their first breaths underwater or head straight from the classroom into the pool if you are taking part in a course.

Seconds from the diving center, heated by solar panels and built to diver training depth and length specifications our pool is not to be missed while you are here.
