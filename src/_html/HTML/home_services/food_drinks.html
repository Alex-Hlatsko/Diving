Exceptional Cuisine

At Pomodoro restaurant we offer our personal rendition of Italian and Egyptian gourmet cuisine with an innovative gastronomic take on traditional cooking methods. Using the finest local and imported produce and the legacy of our love for the exceptional cuisine, we regard it as a personal challenge to create an unforgettable dining experience with our distinctive menu and stand out service in stunning surroundings.

Under the expert guidance of our culinary artist and authentic Italian chef Guiseppe, our team of Chefs uses only the finest and freshest produce when creating our dishes. Handmade pastas, sauces, wood fired crispy pizzas, meats and seafood are only a few of the delights our menu has in store for your eating pleasure.

Meals

Pomodoro chefs are proud to have created a well rounded and balanced menu that is sure to delight every palette. From appetizer through to dessert, every course offers a variety of choices for guests who are sensitive to certain ingredients or are seeking a vegan or vegetarian restaurant.

Be seated by our expert and friendly serving staff and enjoy an aperitif from our imported alcohol menu while you settle in. Pair your meal with wine handpicked by our team that beautifully compliments your menu choice. Indulge in a decadent dessert and sip an authentic Italian Illy Coffee and relax in stunning surroundings.

Sitting

Dine on the terrace which enjoys shade by day and candlelight by night or relax inside in our air-conditioned and non-smoking restaurant.

Enjoy Pomodoro’s exceptional cuisine that has the atmosphere, setting, service and menu that puts us on your list of ‘must’ eats every time you are here.
